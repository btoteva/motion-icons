Animated Font Icons Demo:

icon-heritage-1

Usage:

1.Write element with your selector class for example "icon-motion-example" & add icon-iconname-1 where iconname is the icon you want to display

2.Load Jquery

3.Load icon.motion.min.js or icon.motion.js

4.Usage:

$('.icon-motion-example').iconMotion();

Example:

< body >

< span > class="icon-motion-example icon-heritage-1"></span >
